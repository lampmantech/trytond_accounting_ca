What are the average exchange rates?

Income and deductions

If you need to convert your income or deductions to Canadian dollars, you can get the exchange rates from the Exchange Rates page of the Bank of Canada Web site. It provides exchange rates for various periods (e.g., daily, monthly, annually).

Making payments

For information on making a payment in foreign funds, go to How to make your payment.
